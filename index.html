About

WHO WE ARE

Ubuntu Ability Centre is a non-profit oragnization that nurtures talents and capabilities in adults living with Spina Bifida and/or Hydrocephalus.


BACKGROUND

Ubuntu Ability Centre was established and incorporated in Kenya in August 2010. Our core mandate revolves around healthcare and learning. The following aspects define our keys to success:

I) Self Discovery

We step into our clients' shoes to discover their strengths, weaknesses and interests. These pointers are a guide for empowerment.

II) Placement

From our engagements with dedicated partners and stakeholders, we are better placed to discover great opportunities and link our clients to promote their growth and scalability.

III) Independence

Our exit plan has always been bringing up independent and self-reliant adults despite their physical handicap.

Services

Our clients will benefit from an array of services, depending on their strengths and areas of interest. We help physically challenged persons develop employable skills, from zero to expert. Our courses range from beadwork to programming and a lot in between.

Coding

Our clients are taught computer science fundamentals and acquire programming skills to work remotely as freelancers for companies and startups.

Web Design

Our clients are taught up-to-date web design concepts to help them acquire the architectural skills for designing websites and applications.

Music

Music enthusiasts are well taken care of. Our clients are trained on vocals, song writing and playing instruments to grow in their music careers.

Drama

We help our clients get to the screens in the international spheres by nurturing world class acting and drama skills.

Art work

We nurture the creative nature in our clients to help them learn employable drawing, painting and sketching techniniques.

Beadwork

Our clients are taught how to make market ready jewellery and home decor to sell in the local and international markets.
